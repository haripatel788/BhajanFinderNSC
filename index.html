Welcome to BAPS Bhajan Finder

A project by the Coding Club at National Seva Camp (NSC), built to help devotees search, read, and favorite bhajans—complete with original lyrics, English translations, and embedded audio.

Get Started

Core Features

Search Bhajans

Instantly find bhajans by title, lyrics, or English translation.

Favorites

Save and revisit your favorite bhajans anytime.

Dark / Light Mode

Comfortable viewing day or night with theme toggle.

Realtime DB

Your data syncs instantly via Firebase Realtime Database.

How It Works

1.

Sign Up / Sign In: Create or access your account to manage favorites securely.

2.

Search & Discover: Use the search bar to find bhajans by title, lyrics, or translation.

3.

Favorite & Save: Click the star icon to favorite; stored just for you in the cloud.

4.

Enjoy Anywhere: Access your personalized list from any device, anytime.
